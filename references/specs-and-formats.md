# Ad Specs & Formats Reference

## Ad Formats Overview

### Image Ads
Single static image. Quick to create, ideal for fast campaigns and brand recall.
- Supported objectives: All 6
- Key placements: Feed, Stories, Reels, Marketplace, Right Column, Search, Audience Network

### Video Ads
Dynamic content, most engaging format. Autoplay in feeds (sound off by default).
- Facebook videos can run up to 241 minutes, but keep under 60s for Feed, 15-30s for Reels/Stories
- Always add captions (most users scroll with sound off)
- H.264 compression, square pixels, fixed frame rate, progressive scan, stereo AAC audio 128kbps+

### Carousel Ads
2-10 images/videos in swipeable format. Each card has its own headline, description, URL, and CTA.
- Great for: product catalogs, step-by-step tutorials, feature breakdowns, storytelling
- **Critical rule:** All cards must use the same aspect ratio. Mixing = cropping.
- Mobile users see ~1.75 cards initially, so first card must be compelling
- Videos under 30 seconds loop for ~90 seconds on Facebook; on Instagram carousel, videos may play once and stop

### Collection Ads
Cover image/video + 3-4 product images in a grid. Tapping opens full-screen Instant Experience.
- Pulls from product catalog (Commerce Manager)
- Only 4 products shown on Facebook Feed, 3 on Instagram Feed, 2 on Instagram Stories
- Best for e-commerce with product catalogs

### Stories Ads
Full-screen vertical (9:16) between user stories on Facebook, Instagram, Messenger.
- 24-hour ephemeral feel
- Great for time-sensitive promotions, immersive experiences
- Safe zones critical (see below)

### Reels Ads
Short-form video ads between user Reels.
- Fastest growing placement
- Up to 60 seconds
- Use trending sounds, native font styles, authentic/UGC feel
- Meta's algorithm favors ads that look like organic Reels

### Slideshow Ads
Looping video from up to 15 images. Lightweight alternative to video.

### Instant Experience (formerly Canvas)
Full-screen mobile destination. Loads fast. Can be added to almost any ad format.
- Interactive, multimedia experience
- 90% of mobile time is in-app — this leverages that

### Playable Ads
Interactive preview for mobile apps. Try-before-you-buy experience.
- Available on Facebook and Audience Network

### Flexible Ads
Upload multiple assets; Meta tests combinations and selects the best for each viewer.
- Strong performance in Q4 2025
- Meta frequently displays assets as carousels when given flexibility

## Detailed Specs by Format and Placement

### IMAGE ADS

**Facebook Feed:**
- Format: JPG, PNG
- Ratio: 1:1 (1080×1080) or 4:5 (1080×1350)
- Min resolution: 1080×1080px
- Max file size: 30MB (keep under 1MB)
- Primary text: 125 chars (80 on Facebook before truncation)
- Headline: 40 chars
- Description: 30 chars

**Facebook Stories:**
- Ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- Min resolution: 1080×1920px
- Safe zone: Top 14% (250px) and bottom 20% (340px) free of text/logos
- Primary text: 125 chars
- Headline: 40 chars

**Facebook Reels:**
- Ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- Same safe zone guidelines as Stories

**Facebook Marketplace:**
- Ratio: 1:1
- Min resolution: 1080×1080px
- Headline: 25 chars max (truncated after)

**Facebook Right Column (desktop only):**
- Ratio: 1:1
- Avoid text on images (small display size)
- Headline: 40 chars
- Objectives: Traffic, Engagement, Leads, Sales

**Instagram Feed:**
- Ratio: 1:1 or 4:5 (4:5 grabs more mobile screen space)
- Min resolution: 1080×1080px
- Primary text: 125 chars before truncation

**Instagram Stories:**
- Ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- Safe zone: Top 14%, bottom 35%, 6% each side

**Instagram Explore:**
- Same specs as Feed

**Messenger Inbox:**
- Ratio: 1:1
- Min resolution: 1080×1080px

**Audience Network:**
- Ratio: 1:1
- Min resolution: 1080×1080px
- Primary text: 125 chars
- Headline: 40 chars

### VIDEO ADS

**Facebook Feed:**
- Ratio: 1:1 (1440×1440) or 4:5 (1440×1800) — these take up more mobile space
- Min resolution: 1080×1080px
- Max length: 241 minutes (recommend under 60s)
- Max file size: 4GB
- Captions and sound optional but strongly recommended

**Facebook Stories:**
- Ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- Max length: 120 seconds (recommend 15s)
- Safe zone: Top 14%, bottom 20%

**Facebook Reels:**
- Ratio: 9:16 (1080×1920)
- Max length: 60s (recommend 15-30s)

**Facebook In-Stream:**
- Ratio: 16:9 or 1:1
- Length: 5-15 seconds for skippable; up to 10 min for mid-roll

**Instagram Feed:**
- Ratio: 1:1 or 4:5
- Max length: 60 seconds
- Captions critical

**Instagram Reels:**
- Ratio: 9:16
- Max length: 60 seconds

**Audience Network Rewarded Video:**
- Full-screen video users choose to watch for in-app rewards
- Ratio: 16:9 or 9:16
- Length: 15-30 seconds typical

### CAROUSEL ADS

**All placements (universal specs):**
- Image format: JPG, PNG
- Video format: MP4, MOV, GIF
- Ratio: 1:1 (or 4:5 for Advantage+ catalog ads only — rolling out for others)
- Min resolution: 1080×1080px
- Cards: 2-10 per carousel
- Video length: 1 second to 240 minutes (recommend 15s per card)
- Aspect ratio tolerance: 3%
- All cards MUST use same aspect ratio

**Facebook Feed:**
- Primary text: 80 chars
- Headline: 45 chars per card
- Description: 18 chars per card
- Landing page URL required per card

**Facebook Stories:**
- Cards: 3-10
- Primary text: 125 chars
- Headline: 40 chars
- Safe zone: Top 14%, bottom 20% clear
- **Note:** Video format NOT available for carousel in Facebook Stories

**Facebook Search:**
- Primary text: 125 chars
- Headline: 40 chars
- Description: 20 chars

**Instagram Feed:**
- Same as Facebook Feed specs
- 4:5 portrait available for Advantage+ catalog

**Messenger Stories:**
- Primary text: 80 chars
- Headline: 45 chars
- Description: 18 chars

**Audience Network:**
- Primary text: 125 chars
- Headline: 40 chars
- Description: 20 chars
- Video max: 240 seconds

**Right Column (desktop):**
- Headline: 40 chars
- No text on images (small display)

### COLLECTION ADS
- Cover: Image or video (1:1 ratio)
- Product images from catalog
- Only primary text shows on Instagram Feed
- Only headline shows on Instagram Stories
- Use both primary text AND headline when running across both platforms

## Safe Zones Summary

Critical for Stories, Reels, and any vertical placement:
- **Top:** 14% (~250px) — profile pic, story bar overlap
- **Bottom:** 20-35% (~340-630px) — CTA button, ad text overlay
- **Sides:** 6% each (~65px) — edge overlap

Keep all text, logos, and important design elements INSIDE the safe zone.

## Quick Decision Guide: Which Format?

| Goal | Best Format |
|---|---|
| Single product showcase | Image or Video |
| Multiple products | Carousel or Collection |
| Brand storytelling | Video or Story carousel |
| App install | Video + Playable |
| E-commerce catalog | Collection or Dynamic Carousel |
| Immersive experience | Instant Experience |
| Quick promotion | Stories (image/video) |
| Entertainment/discovery | Reels video |
| Retargeting | Image (product shot) or Carousel |
