# Strategy & Campaign Objectives Reference

## The ODAX Framework (Outcome-Driven Ad Experiences)

Meta consolidated its campaign objectives in 2022 into 6 categories, now standard across all ad accounts. Each objective tells Meta's algorithm what outcome to optimize for.

### 1. Awareness
- **Goal:** Maximize reach and brand recall
- **Encompasses old objectives:** Brand Awareness, Reach, Video Views, Store Traffic
- **Optimization options:** Reach, Ad Recall Lift, ThruPlay (video)
- **Best for:** New brands, product launches, entering new markets, building top-of-funnel audiences
- **Budget guidance:** €15-50/day minimum for meaningful reach
- **Creative tips:** Use short video (<15s), high-impact imagery, clear branding. Visual formats like albums (0.15% engagement) and videos (0.12%) perform best
- **KPIs:** CPM, Reach, Frequency, Ad Recall Lift
- **Pro tip:** Keep targeting broad. Pair with video or story ads for maximum reach. Don't expect direct sales from awareness campaigns.

### 2. Traffic
- **Goal:** Drive visits to website, app, or event
- **Optimization options:** Link clicks, Landing page views
- **Best for:** Blog posts, content marketing, warming audiences for retargeting
- **Caution:** Meta optimizes for clicks, NOT conversions. If your real goal is sales/leads, use those objectives instead
- **KPIs:** CPC, CTR, Landing page views, Cost per landing page view
- **Pro tip:** Always optimize for "Landing page views" over "Link clicks" — the former filters out accidental clicks and bot traffic

### 3. Engagement
- **Goal:** Increase interactions (likes, comments, shares, video views, event responses, messages)
- **Encompasses old objectives:** Engagement, Messages, parts of Video Views and Conversions
- **Now includes:** DM interactions through Messenger, WhatsApp, and Instagram
- **Best for:** Building social proof, community engagement, video view campaigns, starting conversations
- **KPIs:** Post engagement rate, Cost per engagement, ThruPlay rate, Message conversations started
- **Pro tip:** Ask questions, use polls, encourage UGC. Meta favors active interactions (comments, shares) over passive ones (likes)

### 4. Leads
- **Goal:** Capture contact information
- **Formats:** Instant Forms (on-platform), Website conversions, Messenger/WhatsApp conversations
- **Best for:** Service businesses, B2B, consultations, quote requests, newsletter signups
- **Instant Forms best practices:**
  - Keep questions minimal (3-5 fields max)
  - Use prefilled fields where possible
  - Offer clear value proposition (free consultation, discount, guide)
  - Connect to CRM via Zapier or native integration for immediate follow-up
  - Speed of follow-up is critical — contact within 5 minutes for best conversion
- **Lead quality tips:**
  - Add qualifying questions (custom questions, not just name/email)
  - Use "Higher intent" form type (adds a review step)
  - Include a context card explaining what happens after submission
- **KPIs:** CPL (Cost per Lead), Lead quality score, Conversion rate from lead to customer
- **Advantage+ Leads:** New in 2025 — runs leads through the same automated engine as sales campaigns, reducing cost per qualified lead by ~10%

### 5. App Promotion
- **Goal:** Drive app installs or in-app actions
- **Max ad groups:** 9 per Advantage+ app campaign
- **Best for:** Mobile app businesses
- **KPIs:** Cost per install, In-app events, ROAS

### 6. Sales (formerly Conversions)
- **Goal:** Drive purchases or high-value conversions
- **Encompasses old objectives:** Conversions, Catalog Sales
- **Best for:** E-commerce, any business tracking purchase events
- **Requirements:** Needs Pixel + CAPI with sufficient conversion data (50+/week ideal)
- **KPIs:** ROAS, Cost per purchase, Conversion rate, Average order value
- **Pro tip:** This is the most AI-dependent objective. Feed it clean data and diverse creatives. Works best with Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns for e-commerce.

## Campaign Structure Best Practices

### The Three-Tier Hierarchy
```
Campaign (Objective + Budget strategy)
  └── Ad Set (Targeting + Placements + Schedule)
       └── Ad (Creative + Copy + CTA)
```

### Recommended Structures by Business Size

**Small business (€20-50/day):**
- 1 CBO campaign with 2-3 ad sets
- Each ad set: 2-3 creatives
- Let Meta's algorithm do the work
- Focus 70% budget on scale campaign, 30% on testing

**Medium business (€50-500/day):**
- Testing campaign (ABO): Equal budget per ad set for fair comparison
- Scale campaign (CBO): Move winners here
- Optional retargeting campaign
- 3-5 ad sets per campaign max

**Large business (€500+/day):**
- Creative testing campaign (CBO): Isolated concepts per ad set
- Scale campaign (CBO): Proven creatives only
- Optional cost cap campaign for efficiency
- Retargeting campaign (if not using combined audiences)

### The Learning Phase
- Meta needs ~50 optimization events per ad set per week to exit learning
- Avoid significant edits during learning (budget changes >20%, audience changes, creative swaps)
- Learning phase typically lasts 3-7 days
- "Learning Limited" means the ad set isn't getting enough events — consolidate or broaden targeting
- Advertisers keeping <20% of spend in learning phase lower cost per purchase by up to 68%

### Full-Funnel Strategy
1. **Top of funnel (TOFU):** Awareness or Engagement — broad targeting, video/Reels content
2. **Middle of funnel (MOFU):** Traffic or Engagement — retarget video viewers, engagers
3. **Bottom of funnel (BOFU):** Leads or Sales — retarget website visitors, cart abandoners
4. **Post-purchase:** Engagement or Sales — upsell, cross-sell, loyalty

**Modern approach (2025+):** Many top advertisers now combine warm and cold audiences in the same ad set and let the algorithm prioritize. Separate funnel stages are less necessary with Advantage+ automation.

## Advantage+ Suite Overview

Advantage+ is Meta's fully automated suite of AI-powered ad tools:

### Advantage+ Sales Campaigns (ASC)
- Previously called Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns
- Combines prospecting and remarketing in one automated campaign
- Average 22% lift in ROAS
- Grew 70% YoY in Q4 2024, surpassing $20B annual revenue run rate
- Max 150 ads total, capped at 50 per ad set
- Supports: e-commerce, sales, lead generation, and app installs
- Best when: 50+ conversions/week, broad audience, 5-10 diverse creatives, healthy budget

### Advantage+ Audience
- AI-driven targeting that starts with your suggestions but expands beyond
- 13% lower median cost per product catalog sale
- 7% lower median cost per website conversion
- 28% lower average cost per click/lead/landing page view
- Your targeting inputs are "suggestions, not walls"

### Advantage+ Creative
- Auto-optimizes visuals and text
- Can adjust brightness, contrast, aspect ratio
- Generates text variations
- **Caution:** Can sometimes distort brand visuals — monitor and disable specific enhancements if needed

### Advantage+ Placements
- Automatically distributes ads across all available surfaces
- Always use for purchase optimization
- Meta identifies where each creative performs best

### Opportunity Score
- New 0-100 scoring system in Ads Manager
- Evaluates: creative variety, signal quality, audience breadth, conversion event accuracy
- Higher scores correlate with lower CPAs and more stable delivery
- Check regularly for optimization suggestions
